codenames ⓘ

Rules:

  • Find all your team's words: Red has 9, Blue has 8.
  • Spymasters give a one-word clue and a number.
  • Guessers may guess that many words, plus one extra.
  • Wrong guesses end the turn; the Assassin ends the game.
  • You may pass instead of guessing.

Controls:

  • First player in a room becomes admin.
  • Admins promote players by clicking names at the bottom.
  • Admins are underlined; your own name is bolded.
  • Admins reveal a card by clicking it twice.
  • Colored/sorted view is only local to your screen.
